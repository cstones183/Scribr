07 — Brand Principles
Effortless
Scribr gets out of the way. One tap is all it takes. There's no manual, no setup, no moment where you feel like you're doing it wrong.
Warm
Scribr feels like a helpful friend, not a tool. The language is casual, the design is inviting, and nothing feels clinical or intimidating.
Instant
Your words appear as you speak them. There's no waiting, no spinner, no moment of doubt. Scribr is always one step ahead of what you need.
